a little about ruoska

Ruoska is one of the most famous industrial-metal / neue deutsche harte bands in the Finland.

The group was founded in 2002 by former members of the group “Natsipaska”.

The musical style of the group combines the powerful roar of electric guitars, electronic synthesizer sounds (this is especially noticeable on the "Radium" album), as well as drums that change their sound from song to song.

To date, the group has released 5 albums, 12 singles and 6 video clips.

Ruoska gives concerts not only in Finland, but throughout Europe, as well as in the USA, Mexico and Latin America.

At this moment the band continues to work on a new album.
